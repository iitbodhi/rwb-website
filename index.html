We Are the
Ronin Warriors

Born from the spirit of the open road and the soul of Bengal, the Ronin Warriors of Bengal (RWB) is more than a motorcycle club — it is a brotherhood forged in chrome and asphalt.

At our heart beats the TVS Ronin 225 — Bengal's favourite neo-retro machine. With 400+ TVS Ronins in our ranks, backed by 20+ Patron Service Centers and 15+ Service Camps, we don't just ride together — we grow together.

We stand for loyalty, freedom, and the unbreakable bond between rider and road. Every mile is a story. Every sunrise is a new chapter written in exhaust and wind.
